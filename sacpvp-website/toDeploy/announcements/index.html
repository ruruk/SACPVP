We are excited to announce that the V20 Valuation Summit and Conference 2025 will be held in South Africa, following its successful editions in India in 2023 and Brazil in 2024.

This year's theme is 'Valuation for Solidarity, Inclusion, and Sustainability: A Pathway to Global Harmony and Development.'

Event Details: - Date: 15-16 September 2025 - Venue: Cape Town International Convention Centre (CTICC), South Africa - Recommended Accommodation: Hotel Sky

This prestigious event will gather a large number of professionals, including valuation experts, corporate leaders, regulators, and decision-makers from across South Africa and abroad. The conference will offer engaging presentations, insightful discussions, and invaluable networking opportunities aimed at advancing the valuation profession and fostering collaboration.

Sponsorship Categories: - Platinum: R250,000 ($15,000) - Gold: R185,000 ($10,000) - Silver: R95,000 ($5,000) - Bronze: R50,000 ($2,500)

For more information, please contact SAIV Head Office: Tel: 011 642 7266 Email: info@saiv.org.za
