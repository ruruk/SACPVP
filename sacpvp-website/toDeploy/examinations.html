Board Examination Notice - September 2025

Physical/Contact Session
Application Deadline: 30 June 2025 | Exam Date: 17 September 2025
Download PDF

Application Requirements

  • Properly completed application form including the regulated Experience Matrix with supporting documents
  • Certified copies of accredited qualification/s with academic transcript/s (not older than 3 months)
  • Certified copy of ID or passport and valid work permit
  • Updated and detailed curriculum vitae (CV)
  • Letter from supervisor/mentor confirming all valuations were performed under their supervision
  • Practical Work school certificate (compulsory)

Important Notes

  • Applications must be submitted by courier or hand delivery - email applications will NOT be accepted
  • Previous exam candidates who failed do not need to submit new applications
  • Candidates attending practical workschool for the first time in August 2025 will not be allowed to write the exam in September
  • The Board exam will be in a physical/contact session

Eligibility

  • Registered Candidate Valuers who completed their accredited Real Estate qualification
  • Registered Professional Associated who have been registered with the Council for more than 2 years
  • Registered Candidate Valuer registered for 2+ years with 4 years accredited degree can apply for Professional Valuers exam
  • Professional Associated Valuers applying for Professional Valuer exam need accredited four-year degree or diploma with additional qualifications

Examination Fees (VAT Inclusive)

  • Candidates writing Professional Associated Valuer (Pr Aval) examination: R3,700.00
  • Professional Associated Valuer writing the Professional Valuer examination: R4,000.00
  • Payment will be requested once your application has been approved
  • 50% refundable until 14 days before Exam
