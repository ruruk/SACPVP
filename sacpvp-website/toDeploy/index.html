The SACPVP is accepting Board exam applications for 19 March 2026. Submit by courier or hand delivery to Mr. Ntsweng; email submissions are NOT accepted. Closing date: 05 December 2025.

Who can apply: - Registered Candidate Valuers (≥2 years) with accredited Real Estate qualification - Registered Professional Associated Valuers - Pr AVals may apply for Pr Val admission exam if they meet degree and tenure requirements

Application pack includes: - Completed application (incl. Experience Matrix + supporting docs) - Certified qualifications + transcripts (≤3 months old) - Certified ID/passport + valid work permit (if applicable) - Detailed CV - Supervisor/mentor letter (with SACPVP reg no. + postal address) - Practical Workschool certificate (compulsory)

Notes: - Prior unsuccessful candidates and previously approved but unable-to-write candidates should NOT submit new applications—email confirmation before the closing date suffices. - Exam will be held in a physical/contact session.

Fees (VAT incl.; 50% refundable until 14 days before Exam): - Pr AVal exam: R3,700.00 - Pr AVal writing Pr Val exam: R4,000.00 (Payment requested once application is approved.)

About SACPVP

The South African Council for the Property Valuers Profession (SACPVP) is the regulatory body for the property valuation profession in South Africa.

Our mission is to regulate, promote and protect the property valuers profession in the public interest.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and competence within the property valuation industry.

More detailed information about our organization, history, and services will be available on our new website.
