Please find below the detailed directions to the 2025 Practical Workschool venue at Kopanong Hotel. We recommend reviewing these directions before your departure to ensure timely arrival.

From Pretoria: Take the R21 (South) Highway towards OR Tambo International Airport, take the Benoni off-ramp, turn left at the top of the off-ramp, follow the road to the traffic light, at The Engen garage turn left onto the M32, go through three sets of traffic lights and a stop sign. At the stop sign keep straight, drive ± 400 meters and turn right at the next traffic light into Glen Gory Road. After turning right, drive approximately 400 meters and Kopanong Hotel is located on the left hand side.

From Johannesburg: Take the most convenient route towards OR Tambo International Airport (R24 Highway), then follow the R21 (North) Highway towards Pretoria, pass Kempton Park & Atlas off-ramps and take the third off-ramp after the Airport, being Pomona. Turn right at the top of the off-ramp at the traffic light, follow the M32 for 8.5km, (you will go through four sets of traffic lights and a stop sign. At the stop sign keep straight, drive ± 400 meters and turn right at the next traffic light into Glen Gory Road. After turning right, drive approximately 400 meters and Kopanong Hotel is located on the left hand side.

N12 East: Take the N12 to Benoni, pass the East Rand Mall Shopping Centre, and pass the Atlas Road Off-ramp and take the next off-ramp, Tom Jones. Turn left at the traffic light (you are now in Bunyan Road which becomes the Great North Road). Proceed with the Great North Road (3km), at the 7th traffic light (this includes one pedestrian light), turn right into Oak Street. Go through two 4-way stops and pass the Oakfield's Pick'n Pay Shopping Centre on your right hand side. Keep travelling straight on Oak Street, the road name changes to Benoni Road, which becomes Glen Gory Road. From the traffic light at the BP Station, Kopanong Hotel is 4km down the road on your right.

For any queries regarding directions or the venue, please contact the SAIV office.

We are excited to announce that the V20 Valuation Summit and Conference 2025 will be held in South Africa, following its successful editions in India in 2023 and Brazil in 2024.

This year's theme is 'Valuation for Solidarity, Inclusion, and Sustainability: A Pathway to Global Harmony and Development.'

Event Details: - Date: 15-16 September 2025 - Venue: Cape Town International Convention Centre (CTICC), South Africa - Recommended Accommodation: Hotel Sky

This prestigious event will gather a large number of professionals, including valuation experts, corporate leaders, regulators, and decision-makers from across South Africa and abroad. The conference will offer engaging presentations, insightful discussions, and invaluable networking opportunities aimed at advancing the valuation profession and fostering collaboration.

Sponsorship Categories: - Platinum: R250,000 ($15,000) - Gold: R185,000 ($10,000) - Silver: R95,000 ($5,000) - Bronze: R50,000 ($2,500)

For more information, please contact SAIV Head Office: Tel: 011 642 7266 Email: info@saiv.org.za

About SACPVP

The South African Council for the Property Valuers Profession (SACPVP) is the regulatory body for the property valuation profession in South Africa.

Our mission is to regulate, promote and protect the property valuers profession in the public interest.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and competence within the property valuation industry.

More detailed information about our organization, history, and services will be available on our new website.
