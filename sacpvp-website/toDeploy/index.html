The South African Council for the Property Valuers Profession (SACPVP) will host the Board Examination on 25 September 2025. All delegates intending to write must complete and return the Board Examination Venue Form with proof of payment to admin@sacpvp.co.za before 25 September 2025. Payment of the examination fees is mandatory before candidates will be allowed to write.

Examination Fees: - Professional Associated Valuer (Pr AVal): R3 700.00 - Single Residential Property Assessor (SRPA): R3 700.00 - Professional Valuer (Pr Val): R4 000.00

Available Venues: - Cape Town - Durban - Port Elizabeth - Pretoria

Banking Details: - Bank: ABSA Bank - Account No: 21 4028 5741 - Account Type: Cheque account - Branch: Menlyn, 335 645

Please use your surname and registration number as reference when making payment.

7/24/2025
View on job posts

The Alfred Duma Local Municipality has issued External Notice No. 58 of 2025 for the position of Manager: Valuations in the Budget and Treasury Department. This position is open to qualified professionals registered with the South African Council for the Property Valuers Profession (SACPVP).

Position: Manager: Valuations Location: Ladysmith, KwaZulu-Natal Salary: R796,628.17 – R1,034,083.85 per annum (Task Grade 17)

Minimum Requirements: - National Diploma (NQF Level 6) or Degree (NQF Level 7) in Property Valuation or relevant equivalent - Registered as a Professional Valuer or Professional Associated Valuer (no restrictions) - 5 years' experience in municipal valuations - Valid Code B or EB Driver’s License - Strong knowledge of the Municipal Property Rates Act

Key Responsibilities: - Compilation and maintenance of General and Supplementary Valuation Rolls - Property inspections and market valuations for municipal purposes - Attend to valuation objections and appeals - Support in managing the municipal fixed asset register

Closing Date: 08 August 2025, 16:00

About SACPVP

The South African Council for the Property Valuers Profession (SACPVP) is the regulatory body for the property valuation profession in South Africa.

Our mission is to regulate, promote and protect the property valuers profession in the public interest.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and competence within the property valuation industry.

More detailed information about our organization, history, and services will be available on our new website.
