Standards (SAVS)

Professional valuation standards ensuring credibility, transparency, and consistency across South Africa.

Adopted Standards (Downloadable)

The following South African Valuation Standards (SAVS) have been formally adopted by Council and are available for download:

SAVS: 01 International Valuation Standards (IVS)

Adopted

The International Valuation Standards (IVS) provide globally recognized principles and requirements for valuation practice, ensuring that South African valuers operate in line with international norms.

Download Standard (PDF)
Last updated: January 15, 2025

SAVS: 02 Municipal Valuations for Property Rating (MVPR)

Adopted

The Municipal Valuations for Property Rating (MVPR) standard sets out the framework for mass appraisals used in municipal property rating, supporting fair, accurate, and transparent valuation outcomes.

Download Standard (PDF)
Last updated: February 13, 2025

Why Standards Matter

Ensure uniformity and transparency in property valuations.

Protect the public interest by upholding ethical and professional guidelines.

Provide a benchmark for professional valuers, municipalities, and government entities.

Align South Africa with global valuation best practices.
